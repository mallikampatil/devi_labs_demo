Devi Labs

Python, Javascript, and Cybersecurity

Devi-Labs is a non-profit whose mission is to provide accessible and low-cost programming classes to local communities. We strive to educate women and gnc (gender non-conforming) folks about computer science, in a safe supportive environment resulting in their personal growth and empowerment. Our classes are geared toward beginners and are taught by industry professionals who will also act as mentors throughout their technology journey.
